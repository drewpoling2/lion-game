COMBOS
When activated, combos multiply the points you earn, allowing you to accumulate higher scores during the specified duration. Use it strategically to boost your performance and achieve a temporary scoring advantage. Keep in mind that the effect is time-limited, so plan your moves wisely to make the most of this valuable power-up.

Combos can also be stacked. Each combo item has a specified duration. If you already have a combo, and get another one before the current duration runs out, then your current duration timer gets reset to the new combo’s duration limit.

Milestones
The longer you endure, the higher your level climbs, marking each milestone with a corresponding score. Level progression is directly tied to your survival time, where hitting the 1-minute mark propels you to level 2, 2 minutes advances you to level 2, and so on.

At every milestone, you’ll earn a "1 Up," granting you an extra life to extend your gameplay.
Controls
Desktop
JUMP
Press the space bar key to jump in the air and avoid obstacles, or collect items. When in the air, press it again to perform a double jump.
Duck or dive
Press the down arrow key to duck when on the ground. This will reduce your vertical hit box and help you avoid air obstacles.
-Or-
Press the down arrow key to dive when in the air. This will increase your fall speed.
Mobile
JUMP
Tap the screen to jump in the air and avoid obstacles, or collect items. When in the air, tap again to perform a double jump.
Duck or dive
Drag your finger downward on the screen to duck when on the ground. This will reduce your vertical hit box and help you avoid air obstacles.
-Or-
Drag your finger downward on the screen to dive when in the air. This will increase your fall speed.
