Collect coins & gem tokens to boost your score. Points count toward lvl experience & score. Coins boost score instantly. Gems are stored & their points are gained at the end of the game based on the players grade bonus.

Items
These randomly drop and give either timed or instant power up. They can either be in a cloud, or a breakable crate.
Star
Makes the Lion invincible to enemy hits for a short period of time. During this period, enemies Lion touches fall over instantly. Every enemy the lion touches gives points incrementing by 2x every time.
Rarity: common
Duration: 8s
MAGNET
Makes all collectables on the screen at the moment get pulled into the lion.
Rarity: common
Duration: 8s
Elm Leaf
Gives Lion slowfall, and gives lion unlimited jump limit for short time.
Rarity: rare
Duration: 3s
Sundae
Gives Lion +1 life
Rarity: epic
Duration: 1s
Power Ups
The longer you endure, the higher your level climbs, and the more power ups you can get. Upon your first level up, you get to choose a starter power up that is very impactful. Following level ups give you the choice between less impactful power ups, that are the same every time.

Controls
Desktop
JUMP
Press the space bar key to jump in the air and avoid obstacles, or collect items. When in the air, press it again to perform a double jump.
Duck or dive
Press the down arrow key to duck when on the ground. This will reduce your vertical hit box and help you avoid air obstacles.
-Or-
Press the down arrow key to dive when in the air. This will increase your fall speed.
Mobile
JUMP
Tap the screen to jump in the air and avoid obstacles, or collect items. When in the air, tap again to perform a double jump.
Duck or dive
Drag your finger downward on the screen to duck when on the ground. This will reduce your vertical hit box and help you avoid air obstacles.
-Or-
Drag your finger downward on the screen to dive when in the air. This will increase your fall speed.
